The process of how Jeff and Kristy made the Flash Movie Shi are….
  1. We came up with the idea
  2. Storyboard was created
  3. Had a photo shoot
  4. Picked the photos we wanted to use
  5. Cleaned up the photos we wanted to use (in Photoshop)
  6. Turned the photos into Jpegs
  7. Picked out the Font Face
  8. Made all the red text for the flash move (in Flash)
  9. Imported the Jpegs into Flash
  10. Animated the photos in Flash by moving them across the stage
  11. Added transitions into the flash movie (in Flash)
  12. Made a green screen and the credits for the flash movie (in Flash)
  13. Put the green screen and the credits at the beginning and ending of the flash movie
  14. Found sound that would fit the speed and mood of the movie
  15. Compressed sound for the flash movie
  16. Added the sound
  17. Turned the flash movie into a SWF (sock wave flash)
