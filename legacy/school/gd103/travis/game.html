"Gorillaz" Video Game Concept

Titled: "Gravity Gorilaz"

Video Game Type: Racing RPG (Role Playing Game)

Number of Players: 1 to 4

Platform: PS2

Gravity Gorilaz is a racing RPG similar to SuperMarioCart with a race time limit. Consisting of 5 main character/vehicles choices, 4 hidden characters/vehicles, 7 main race tracks and 3 hidden race tracks. Gravity Gorilaz Story: The Gorillaz Band have to make it to their concerts in various cities befor time runs out for them and their friend Bunny who has mysteriously dissapeared. Its up to the player to get them to their concerts and help find Bunny. Gravity Gorilaz characters consist of Russel, Murdoc, 2-d, Noodle, and Gorilla Gorilla. Gravity Gorilaz bonus characters include Chimp, Genie-Russel, Dracula-Murdoc and Bunny. Character Vehicles: Murdoc drives a Winnebago, special weapon is the Septic Spill; which, acts as an oil slick. 2-d drives a large Ford sedan, special weapon is the Tire Shredders; which, stop the opponent for several seconds. Russel drives a large pimped out Cadillac, special weapon is the Hydrolic crush; which, drops on top of the opponents car. Noodle drives a small Honda Civic type vehicle, special weapon is the Electric Bumper; which, electrifies the opponent on contact. Gorilla Gorilla drives a large Van, special weapon is the Banana Bombs; which, stun and knock the opponent. Chimp drives a Scooter, special weapon is Battery Acid; which, burns the opponents tires, slowing them down. Genie-Russel drives a Gold version of, his Cadillac, special weapon is the Hydrolic Bounce; which, causes earthquake like vibrations. Dracula-Murdoc drives a Hearse, special weapon is the Stake Stopper; which are shot at opponents tires to disable them. Bunny drives a Pink School Bus, special weapon is the Aerosol attack; which, makes a smoke screen. Gravity Gorilaz Bonus Breakdown. Once the player has beaten the game on normal with one character a new race track will open. The hidden characters are given when the player has beaten the game on hard difficulty with specific characters. Bunny will become available only after all tracks and other characters have been found. In addition to the bonus race tracks and characters there will be hidden Gorillaz music tracks available after the player uses Bunny to win the races.
