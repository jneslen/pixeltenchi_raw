Gorillaz Game Concept

Game Name: Gorillaz

Platform: Playstation 2

Story Line: The four members of the Gorillaz rock group have come across a problem. An evil mechanical ape named "Osmos" has a plot to take over the world with his evil army of "mecha-apes." However, only the Gorillaz group has knowledge of this evil plot due to the fact that Osmos has cleverly kept his intentions secret. The Gorillaz had known Osmos from years before and fell suspicious after peculiar actions that Osmos was making. Osmos' Plan: Take over the world one continent at a time through means of military use of his massing army of mecha-apes.

Goal of Game: To Stop Osmos by use of the four members of the group Gorillaz and their individual special skills. The Gorillaz must respond to a series of attacks being conducted on the seven continents of the world.

Character Skills: Each member of the Gorillaz has strengths and weaknesses in their skills in certain aspects of their need to participate in stopping Osmos and his evil army. Each member will have different weapons and vehicles for the level of each continent. Continents/Levels-

Antarctica: Osmos has established a hidden base in the vast ocean of ice in Antarctica. Your job is to work your way across the continent in order to find and destroy the hidden base through use of military modified snowmobiles, Snow-Cats, and other snow-based vehicles.

Africa: Osmos has lead you into a trap in which the Gorillaz must get out of and find a weapon that will help in destroying Osmos later on. Your responsibility is to move through a series of mazes in ancient Egypt as well as desert caverns destroying mecha-apes as they try to stop your progress.

South America: In the Jungles of South America you must stop the destruction of forests and stop the spread of Osmos army on local villages through the use of weaponry and other equipment that you may have picked up along the way in other continents.

Australia: Osmos has a secret factory that is producing massive amounts of mecha-apes located under the water in the Great Barrier Reef. Your job will be to find your way through the underwater mazes by use of a select variety of submersive vehicles.

Europe: Osmos has control over the continent through use of a secret weapon similar to a powerful laser that can kill a person with little effort or resistance. You must sneak into the compound where this weapon and smaller versions of this weapon are being held. You must sneak up on mecha-apes without being seen and destroy them before they destroy you.

Asia: The governments of Asia have no idea that Osmos has migrated himself to their and has taken refuge in one of the major cities on the Pacific Rim. You must find Osmos through a series of clues he has left in several large cities such as Tokyo, Hong Kong, ect… be prepared for several shootouts in the streets of these cities with mecha-ape assassins. You will meet Osmos and oust him from the area. Then pursue him to the final continent North America.

North America: Osmos has fled Asia, but is trying to get away in his supersonic fighter jet. You must seek and destroy him in your own Gorillaz Jet. Along your pursuit destroy as many Mecha-ape fighters you can to ensure an easier aerial fight against Osmos. Prologue: The Gorillaz have defeated Osmos and have been given the attention of the world. Then return home to continue their dream of playing their music.
